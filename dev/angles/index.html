Angles

Type for easily working with angles in DMS format and converting to/from decimal.

CelestialCalc.Angle — Type
Angle

Represents an angle with degrees, minutes and seconds. The isnegative flag is used instead of allowing degrees to be negative for better dealing with negative angles smaller than 1 (example: -0°10'10''). For convenience, the constructors accept negative degrees, but the final object always has positive degrees and sets the isnegative as needed.

Examples

julia> Angle(10,5,30)
10°05'30.00''

julia> Angle(45,15,2,true)
-45°15'02.00''

julia> Angle(-45,15,2)
-45°15'02.00''

julia> Angle(270,55,2.1)
270°55'02.10''
